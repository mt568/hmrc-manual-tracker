title: 'Averaging: successive claims'
---

S222(2), (3), S222A(3), (4) Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005

A year can be averaged more than once under either two-year or five-year claims.

Examples of these are given at [BIM84210](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim84210) onwards.
