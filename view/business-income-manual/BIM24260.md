If a company pays dividends, it will normally be liable to tax as a trading concern. However, if the other conditions in [BIM24255](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24255) are satisfied, there is likely to be liability only where there is a marked inequality of shareholdings and the dividends are substantial.
