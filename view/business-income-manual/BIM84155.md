title: 'Averaging: claims: time limit'
---

S222(5), S222A(6) Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005

A claim for averaging must be made before the end of the first anniversary of 31 January following the year of claim. So a claim for averaging in 2016/2017 must be made by 31 January 2019.

See, however, [BIM84150](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim84150) regarding certain adjustments to assessments for earlier years and where a late claim is made in an enquiry case.

Withdrawal of claims may be allowed within the normal time limit.
