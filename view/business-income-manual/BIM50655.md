title: 'Athletes: Lottery funding: Athlete Personal Awards: Types of award'
---

Athlete Personal Awards are designed to act as a contribution towards the living costs and the sporting costs of the athlete. Most athletes will require income from other sources (see [BIM50610](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim50610)).

Awards may be paid over a period of up to four years. Broadly the amount of each award depends on:

* category of performance level: A, B or C. Maximum awards within each of these three categories are banded according to status - senior (aged 21 or over). and under-21
* personal means of the recipient. Awards will be reduced, after a disregard, by the amount of any other income (such as income from an employment).
