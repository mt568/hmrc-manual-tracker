title: 'Trade losses - restriction of relief: larger undertaking'
---

You should not deny relief in respect of a loss in a trade which is carried on on a commercial basis but not carried on with a view to the realisation of profits where that trade forms a part of a larger undertaking and the undertaking as a whole is carried on with a view to profit.
