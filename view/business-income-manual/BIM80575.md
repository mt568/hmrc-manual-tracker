title: 'Computing the amount to assess: business changes: cessation - collection of
  trade debts'

A trade may have been permanently discontinued notwithstanding the fact that trade debts remain to be collected (**Tryka Ltd v Newall**(1963) 41 TC 146 at page 158, distinguishing tax law from bankruptcy law).
