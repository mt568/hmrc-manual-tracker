title: 'Farming quotas: compensation for permanent loss of quotas'
---

S33 Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005, S53 Corporation Tax Act 2009

Any compensation received for the permanent withdrawal or surrender of all or part of a farmer’s quotas is compensation for the permanent loss of a fixed capital asset. The sums received are capital receipts (see [CG77920](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/capital-gains-manual/cg77920) onwards).
