## Allow in the period when the loss is incurred or determined

Any deduction in respect of a loss should normally be allowed in arriving at the profits of the period in which the loss is incurred or its amount determined. There is general guidance on timing issues at [BIM42200](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim42200) onwards.
