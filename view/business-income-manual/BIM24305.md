title: 'Meaning of trade: mutual trading and members clubs: members'' clubs: interest
  paid or received'
---

Where a members’ club pays interest on loans from brewery companies, or other suppliers, it may need to account for tax under SS945-960 Income Tax Act 2007.

A members’ club is liable to tax for any saving and investment income it receives.

You should bear these points in mind when considering the liability of members’ clubs.
