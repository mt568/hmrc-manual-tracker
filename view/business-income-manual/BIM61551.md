title: "Particular trades: Lloyd\u2019s underwriters"
---

Lloyd’s underwriters are not dealt with in local offices. For details of the offices dealing with taxation of Lloyd’s underwriters, see the Lloyd’s Manual.

Lloyd’s underwriters are excluded from using the cash basis (see [BIM70000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim70000))
