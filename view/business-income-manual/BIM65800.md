The guidance in these pages relates to solicitors practising as self-employed individuals or as partners in a partnership or limited liability partnership. It does not cover companies authorised to provide legal services under the Solicitors Regulation Authority rules or solicitors working as employees.
- [BIM65801 — Bank interest received: Liability to tax](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim65801)
- [BIM65805 — Bank interest received: Solicitors’ rules](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim65805)
- [BIM65810 — Interest received on behalf of non-resident clients](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim65810)
- [BIM65815 — Duty to deduct tax on interest paid to a client](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim65815)
- [BIM65820 — Requirement to provide details of interest to HMRC](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim65820)
- [BIM65830 — Temporary loans and guarantees](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim65830)
