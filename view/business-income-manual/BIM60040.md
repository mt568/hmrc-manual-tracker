title: 'Measuring the profits (particular trades): land: trading transactions: deal
  or investment?'
---

In practice, it will be rare for someone to say that they are starting a trade in property. Intention may be reasonably inferred from all the circumstances at the time. If you identify the badges of trade in a transaction, these will indicate a trading intention.

It is necessary to examine the transaction objectively. Do the facts suggest a ‘deal’ or an investment?

If, notwithstanding the purchaser’s stated intentions, the facts suggest a trading intention it should be argued that the transaction is part of a trade.
