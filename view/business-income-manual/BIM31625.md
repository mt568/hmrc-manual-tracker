Claims for recovery of overpaid tax (Sch1AB Taxes Management Act 1970) for periods where VAT assessments have become final and conclusive should be decided using the overpayment relief legislation and guidance. See [SACM12000+](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/self-assessment-claims-manual/sacm12000) for details.

Any other cases of difficulty should be submitted to Business Profits.
