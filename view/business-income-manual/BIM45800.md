S58, S59 Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005

## Income Tax

A statutory deduction in computing the profits of a trade applies to the incidental costs of raising loan finance which would otherwise not be an allowable deduction. The rules apply only to Income Tax.

There are special rules regarding the incidental costs of raising loan finance for businesses using the cash basis - see [BIM70040](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim70040)

## Corporation Tax

For Corporation Tax, incidental costs of raising loan finance are dealt with under the loan relationships rules. For details see [CFM30000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/corporate-finance-manual/cfm30000) onwards.
