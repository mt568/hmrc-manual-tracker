title: 'Measuring the profits (particular trades): land: trading transactions: outline'
---

There is an overview of the subject at [BIM60010](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60010).

The relevant principles to be applied are set out in [BIM60020](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60020) - [BIM60085](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60085).

Particular situations are considered at the following references:

* sale of land acquired by inheritance or gift ([BIM60090](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60090)),
* purchase and resale of superior interest by tenant ([BIM60095](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60095) - [BIM60130](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60130)),
* sales of timeshare ([BIM60135](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60135) - [BIM60155](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60155)).

Notes on related topics are to be found at the following references-

* computational etc points relating to established building, developing and land dealing trades ([BIM51500](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim51500) onwards),
* sale and lease back ([BIM61300](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61300) onwards),
* transactions in land: capital gains treated as income ([BIM60300](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60300) onwards),
* list of useful land dealing cases and the principles they demonstrate ([BIM60160](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60160)),
* summary of occasions on which a report to Business Profits regarding land trading transactions is required ([BIM60165](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60165)).

‘Land’ in the following paragraphs includes any buildings and other structures, land covered by water and any estate, and interest in land, in accordance with the definition used in the Interpretation Act 1978.
