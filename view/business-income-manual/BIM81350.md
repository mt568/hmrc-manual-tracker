title: 'Transitional rules: averaging profits of farmers and creative artists '
---

Transition profits should not be taken account in determining “the relevant profits” for the purposes of averaging profits of farmers and creative artists in Chapter 16 of Part 2 of ITTOIA 2005 (see [BIM84000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim84000) for guidance on averaging profits).
