## S205 Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005

Any overlap relief not used on a change of accounting date (see [BIM81090](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim81090)) is given in full in the tax year in which the business ceases.

### Example

The business in the example at [BIM81090](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim81090) ceases on 31 May 2020. The overlap profit available for relief is £25,002.

The basis periods for the final 2 years are:

| **Year** | - | - |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2019-2020 | Year 10 | 12 months to 31 October 2019 |
| 2020-2021 | Final year | 7 months from 1 November 2019 to 31 May 2020 |

The profits for the 7 months to 31 May 2020 are £80,000. Overlap relief for the remaining overlap profits (£25,002) is deducted to give taxable profits for 2020-2021 of £54,998.

The total profits taxed over the life of this business will exactly equal the total profits made.
