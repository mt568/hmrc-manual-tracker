title: 'Specific deductions: professional fees: capital compensation claims'
---

Fees are capital if incurred on:

* pursuing debts of a capital nature
* disputing the amount of a capital expense
* pursuing claims for capital compensation (e.g. fees in relation to compensation claimed under the Town and Country Planning Acts in respect of land held as a fixed asset are capital expenditure (see [BIM51630](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim51630)))
