title: 'Leasing: avoidance: sale and leaseback of land: liability of recipient not
  affected'
---

It should be noted that an adjustment under the guidance at [BIM61301](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61301) onwards affects only the lessee and does not affect the liability of the recipient of a payment in respect of land.
