- [BIM34105 — Adjustment income and expenses: expenses already allowed](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34105)
- [BIM34110 — Adjustment income and expenses: stock and depreciation](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34110)
- [BIM34115 — Adjustment income and expenses: change from realisation basis to mark to market (fair value) accounting](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34115)
- [BIM34120 — Adjustment income and expenses: change from realisation basis to mark to market (fair value) accounting: spreading election](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34120)
- [BIM34130 — Adjustment income and expenses: calculation of adjustment](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34130)
- [BIM34135 — Adjustment income and expenses: meanings](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34135)
