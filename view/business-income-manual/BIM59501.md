title: 'Particular trades: internet trading '
---

Online auctions provide opportunities for selling which may or may not amount to trading. Guidance on internet trading is covered in ‘A guide for people who sell items online, through classifieds and at car boot sales’ available on the GOV.UK website at [Selling goods or services on a digital platform - GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/selling-goods-or-services-on-a-digital-platform)
