title: 'Builders, property dealers & developers: Valuation: not negotiated valuation:
  accepted'
---

If the District Valuer accepts the taxpayer’s valuation you will receive a report agreeing the valuation ‘as returned’. In such cases, you should compute the profit or loss and conclude any enquiry or determine any appeal on the basis of that valuation.
