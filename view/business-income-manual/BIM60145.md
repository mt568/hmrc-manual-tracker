title: 'Measuring the profits (particular trades): land: trading transactions: timeshare
  schemes - builders and developers'
---

Where a builder or trading developer constructs and sells timeshares, we consider that the timeshare receipts should be charged as trading receipts. The developer is merely exploiting land in the most advantageous manner.
