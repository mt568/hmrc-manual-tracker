title: 'Meaning of trade: mutual trading and members clubs: members'' clubs: commission
  income'

The proprietor of vending or fruit machines, or electronic games, will often pay a commission to a members’ club for the right to install machines on the club’s premises. These commissions are not assessable on the club as either trading income or miscellaneous income.
