title: "Foreign exchange: business uses the \u2018offset method\u2019"
---

The ‘offset’ or ‘cover’ method applies to companies which have adopted SSAP 20 Foreign Currency Translation under old UK GAAP. Its use is subject to certain conditions, which are discussed at [CFM26130](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/corporate-finance-manual/cfm26130).

See [CFM26140](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/corporate-finance-manual/cfm26140) for an example of the use of this method.
