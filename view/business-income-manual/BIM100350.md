This chapter deals with anti-avoidance provisions aimed at arrangements where an individual gives up the prospect of future income and receives instead a ‘capital amount’. The chapter contains the following:
- [BIM100351 — Introduction](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100351)
- [BIM100355 — Conditions needed](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100355)
- [BIM100360 — Occupation](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100360)
- [BIM100365 — Capital amount](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100365)
- [BIM100370 — Tax year](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100370)
- [BIM100375 — Deduction of tax from payments](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100375)
- [BIM100380 — Submit to Counter-Avoidance first](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100380)
