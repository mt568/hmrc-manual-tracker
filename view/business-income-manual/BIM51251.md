## Betting, Gaming and Lotteries Act 1963

The contribution payable by a bookmaker to the Horserace Betting Levy Board under the Betting, Gaming and Lotteries Act 1963 (see [BIM58801](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim58801)) should be allowed as a deduction in computing his trade profits for tax purposes. The contribution is payable in respect of a levy period which is defined as ‘a period of twelve months beginning with the first day of April in each year’.
