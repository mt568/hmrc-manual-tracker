title: 'Receipts: general: possible future repayment'
---

It should be noted that the mere fact that income may, in certain later circumstances, have to be repaid, does not preclude its treatment as a trade receipt (Smart v Lincolnshire Sugar Co Ltd [1937] 20 TC 643).

Guidance specific to the treatment of commissions received by insurance brokers on ’indemnity terms' is given at [BIM40680](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40680).
