title: 'Meaning of trade: mutual trading and members clubs: mutual associations: specific
  activities: mutual health insurers: two interrelated questions'
---

You need to consider two interrelated questions:

* whether the mutual health insurer is carrying on a trade, and
* if the mutual health insurer is trading, the extent to which that trade is mutual trading.

The absence of a profit-seeking motive does not preclude a finding of trading - see [BIM24045](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24045).
