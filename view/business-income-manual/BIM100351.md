title: 'Sale of income by an individual in exchange for capital: introduction'

S773-S789 Income Tax Act 2007

There are anti-avoidance provisions which are aimed at arrangements where an individual gives up the prospect of future income but, either he or some other person, receives instead a ‘capital amount’.
