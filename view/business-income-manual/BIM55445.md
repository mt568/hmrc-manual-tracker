title: 'Farming: stock valuation: shares in animals'
---

S30 Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005, S50 Corporation Tax Act 2009

Where a farmer owns a share in an animal, the share should be treated as trading stock unless it is a production animal of a class for which the farmer has made a herd basis election, in which case see [BIM55635](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim55635).
