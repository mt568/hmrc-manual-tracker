Guidance on:

* The commercial background and accountancy treatment of leasing is in the Business Leasing Manual at [BLM00001](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-leasing-manual/blm00001) onwards.
* The taxation of leases is at [BLM30000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-leasing-manual/blm30000) onwards, [BLM40000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-leasing-manual/blm40000) onwards and [BLM50000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-leasing-manual/blm50000) onwards.
* Anti-avoidance legislation on leased trading assets and leased capital sums is at [BIM61200](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61200) onwards.
* Anti-avoidance legislation on sale and leaseback of land is at [BIM61300](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61300) onwards.
* Income from property is in the Property Income Manual ([PIM](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/property-income-manual/pim)).
