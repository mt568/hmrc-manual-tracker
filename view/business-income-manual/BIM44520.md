title: 'Specific deductions: employee benefit trusts: used with retirement benefit
  schemes'
---

Employers may use EBTs in conjunction with schemes to provide their employees with pension entitlements and other retirement benefits.

Guidance on tax deductions for employers’ contributions to retirement benefit schemes is at [BIM46000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46000) onwards, and in the [Pensions Tax Manual](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/pensions-tax-manual).
