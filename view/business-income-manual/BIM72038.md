S33A(4)(f) ITTOIA 2005

In calculating the profits of a trade on the cash basis, no
deduction is allowed for a financial asset.

A financial asset means any right under or in connection
with

* a
  financial instrument
* an
  arrangement that is capable of producing a return that is economically
  equivalent to a return produced under any financial instrument.

An arrangement includes any agreement, understanding,
scheme, transaction, or series of transactions (whether or not legally
enforceable).
