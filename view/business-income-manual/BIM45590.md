title: 'Specific deductions: insurance: premiums paid to mutual concerns'
---

Insurance premiums otherwise admissible as a deduction should not be regarded as inadmissible on the grounds that they are paid to an insurance association that is a mutual concern (see Thomas v Richard Evans and Co Ltd. [1927] 11 TC 790).
