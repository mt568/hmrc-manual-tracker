The old reliefs for expenditure on films summarised at [BIM56010](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim56010) led to a high number of avoidance schemes. Anti-avoidance legislation which may still be relevant in practice and which deals only with those old film reliefs is dealt with in this chapter. The guidance is arranged as follows.

- [BIM56525 — Individual exit schemes: exit events](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim56525)
