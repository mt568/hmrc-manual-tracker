S34 Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005, S54 Corporation Tax Act 2009

## Layout of guidance

The issue of duality has been considered in a variety of non-travel cases. When considering the following examples you should bear in mind the general guidance at [BIM37007](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37007).
- [BIM37660 — Duality of, or non-trade, purpose: non travel topics: subsistence](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37660)
- [BIM37665 — Duality of, or non-trade, purpose: non travel topics: accommodation and subsistence](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37665)
- [BIM37670 — Duality of, or non-trade, purpose: non travel topics: accommodation and subsistence: 'itinerant' trades](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37670)
- [BIM37675 — Duality of, or non-trade, purpose: non travel topics: accommodation and subsistence: sub-contractors](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37675)
- [BIM37677 — Renting accommodation while living away from home](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37677)
- [BIM37680 — Duality of, or non-trade, purpose: non travel topics: interest payments](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37680)
- [BIM37690 — Duality of, or non-trade, purpose: non travel topics: building society demutualisation](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim37690)
