title: 'Specific deductions: hire purchase: repairs and renewals'
---

The costs of repairs and renewals to a ’hire purchase’ asset are allowable as a deduction. Guidance on repair and renewals generally is at [BIM46900](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46900) onwards.
