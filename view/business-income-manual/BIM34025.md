title: 'Change of basis of computing taxable profits: from invalid to valid basis:
  contents'
---

Where a business has been using an invalid basis of computing profits and changes to a valid basis the profits for any affected periods of account must be computed on the new valid basis. This is covered in the following two pages:
- [BIM34030 — Invalid to valid basis: computational adjustment](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34030)
- [BIM34035 — Invalid to valid basis: approach to earlier years](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim34035)
