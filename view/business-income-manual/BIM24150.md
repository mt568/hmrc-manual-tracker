## Introduction and layout of guidance

As explained in [BIM24001](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24001) there have historically been three separate strands to the umbrella topic of mutual trading:

* mutual insurance
* other trading activities, and
* social and recreational activities.

The first two involve mutual trading whereas the third category strictly does not. The guidance also follows this approach. Guidance on mutual insurance is in the General Insurance Manual at [GIM9000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/general-insurance-manual/gim9000) onwards. The following guidance covers:
- [BIM24155 — Non-mutual insurance trading activities: corporation water works](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24155)
- [BIM24160 — Non-mutual insurance trading activities: corn trading association](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24160)
