title: Trade losses - claims to relief
---

Relief for trade losses requires a formal claim. The legislation and practice that applies to all claims and elections specified by the Taxes Acts are set out in the Self Assessment Claims Manual (SACM). The information in [BIM85070](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim85070)-[BIM85090](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim85090) should be read in conjunction with SACM.

For information on making and amending claims and supplementary claims, see [SACM3000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/self-assessment-claims-manual/sacm3000) onwards.

Guidance on late claims is at [SACM10030](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/self-assessment-claims-manual/sacm10030)
