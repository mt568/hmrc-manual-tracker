Trade associations are within the charge to Corporation Tax. This is so whether or not they have adopted the HMRC arrangement referred to in [BIM24805](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24805) onwards.

It is important to recognise from the outset that the HMRC arrangement is only appropriate in those cases where the subscriptions in question are not otherwise chargeable to Corporation Tax - that is to say the activities of the trade protection association do not amount to the carrying on of a trade. Whether activities amount to a trade is essentially a question of fact. There is more on this at [BIM24045](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24045).
