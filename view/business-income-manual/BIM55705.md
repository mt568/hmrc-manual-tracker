title: 'Farming: stud farms: open market valuations of thoroughbred horses'
---

Specialist advice on open market valuations of thoroughbred horses is available from the Bloodstock Section, Shares and Assets Valuation, Nottingham. Requests for advice should include

* the name(s) of the horse(s) to be valued
* the valuation dates
* any opinion of open market value together with reasoning, if supplied.
