title: "Trading income: interpretation - \u2018trade\u2019 or \u2018business\u2019"
---

Where the word ‘trade’ or the word ‘business’ is used in this manual it should be read, for Income Tax purposes, as covering professions and vocations except in [BIM20055](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim20055) onwards and elsewhere where the context requires.
