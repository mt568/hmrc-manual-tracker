title: 'Specific deductions: patents and royalties: contents'

This chapter deals with the deductions available in calculating the profits of a trade in connection with patents, know-how, copyright, trade marks and design right. It contains the following:
- [BIM45951 — Costs of obtaining or extending patent rights](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim45951)
- [BIM45955 — Expenditure on acquisition of know how](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim45955)
- [BIM45960 — Costs of registering, or extending the life of, trade marks and designs](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim45960)
- [BIM45965 — Payment of royalties etc.](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim45965)
