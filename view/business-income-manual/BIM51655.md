title: 'Builders, property dealers & developers: private street works: payments to
  local authorities: interest'
---

Interest allowed by the local authority on amounts paid to it under [BIM51645](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim51645) is interest from which Income Tax is deductible (see [CTM40850](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/company-taxation-manual/ctm40850) onwards). The interest is income in the hands of the recipient.

Where interest is appropriated by the local authority towards the cost of the street works, the appropriation should be regarded as constituting a payment of interest to the person entitled to it.
