title: "TMIA \u2013 Other Considerations: Post-Cessation Receipts"
---

Post cessation receipts do not qualify for the trading allowance as they are not chargeable under Chapter 2 Part 2 ITTOIA05. Therefore, the trading allowance cannot be used to reduce an individual’s gross income from post cessation receipts.

More information on post-cessation receipts can be found at [BIM90030](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim90030) to [BIM90075](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim90075) inclusive.

More information on post-cessation expenses can be found at [BIM90080](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim90080) to [BIM90135](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim90135) inclusive.
