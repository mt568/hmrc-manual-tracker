title: 'Care providers: shared lives carers: transitional year 2010-11'
---

For 2010-11 only, shared lives carers could choose between the simplified arrangements at [BIM52800](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim52800) onwards and qualifying care relief (see [BIM52753](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim52753) onwards) if they would meet the requirements for both.
