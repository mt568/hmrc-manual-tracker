title: 'Transactions in land: Period in which the gain is taxed'
---

S758 Income Tax Act 2007, S818 Corporation Tax Act 2010

The gain is taxable under the transactions in land provisions as income of the person chargeable (see [BIM60325](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60325) and [BIM60328](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60328)) in the:

* tax year in which the gain arises (where the person liable for the tax is an individual, a trustee or a personal representative), or
* accounting period in which the gain arises (where the person liable for the tax is a company).

For guidance on how to compute the amount to be taxed, see [BIM60333](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60333).

For details of where the tax due may be recovered from another person, see [BIM60330](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim60330).
