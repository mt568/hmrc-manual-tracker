title: 'Transactions in land: Definitions: Developed'
---

Whether the land has been developed is a question of fact.

‘Development’ for this purpose is not defined, but is interpreted to mean any physical adaptation or preparation for a new use.

The obtaining of planning permission does not constitute physical development.
