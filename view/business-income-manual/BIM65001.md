title: Prostitution
---

If the activities of a prostitute or any other person deriving income from prostitution are organised in such a way as to constitute a trade or profession (see [BIM20000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim20000) onwards), the profits are liable to Income Tax. This was confirmed by CIR v Aken [1990] 63TC395.

If you are investigating such profits you must refer to EM2097.
