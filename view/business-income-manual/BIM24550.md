It follows from first principles that distributions out of the surpluses from mutual trading to members by a continuing mutual concern (corporate or non-corporate) are trading receipts where the member who receives them does so because of the trade they carry on - see [BIM24555](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24555). In addition there are two statutory provisions:

## S1070 Corporation Tax Act 2010

There is guidance at [BIM24565](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24565).

## S104 Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005, S101 Corporation Tax Act 2009

There is detailed guidance at [BIM24600](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24600) onwards.
