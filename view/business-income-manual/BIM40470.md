title: 'Specific receipts: grants and subsidies: Highlands and Islands Enterprise'
---

Highlands and Islands Enterprise provides financial assistance by way of loans and grants towards specified expenditure. These should be dealt with as at [BIM40455](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40455).

In addition, Highlands and Islands Enterprise makes special grants which are not earmarked to any particular expenditure, capital or revenue. Exceptionally, these special grants may be treated as non-revenue receipts. This follows an agreement made in respect of grants paid by the Highlands and Island Development Board, the predecessor body to Highlands & Islands Enterprise.
