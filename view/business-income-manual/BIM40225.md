## Where to find further specific guidance

Unclaimed balances may be conveniently divided into four broad categories:

1. receipts that become taxable receipts by operation of law (see [BIM40230](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40230))
2. overpayments (see [BIM40240](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40240))
3. holding of funds that always belong to someone else (see [BIM40250](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40250))
4. trade debts that are never paid (see [BIM40255](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40255))
