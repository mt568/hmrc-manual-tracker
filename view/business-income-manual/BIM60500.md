The provisions were enacted in Finance Act 1969 and only a small number of cases have proceeded to the Courts. They are:

Diversion schemes:

* Yuill v Wilson [1980] 52 TC 674
* Yuill v Fletcher [1984] 58 TC 145
* Sugarwhite v Budd [1988] 60 TC 679

‘Slice of the action’ contracts:

* Page v Lowther [1983] 57 TC 199
* Newman v Pepper and Newman v Morgan [2000] STC (SCD) 345

Other transactions:

* Byfield v Edwards and Winterton v Edwards [1979] 52 TC 655
* Chilcott and others v CIR [1981] 55 TC 446
* Pardoe v Entergy Power Development Corporation [2000] 72 TC 617
