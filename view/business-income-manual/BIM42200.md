This chapter considers when an allowable deduction should be deducted in computing trade profits.

{For the rules regarding timing of deductions for a business using the cash basis, see [BIM70030](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim70030)}.

It contains the following:
- [BIM42201 — Date allowable](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim42201)
- [BIM42205 — No specific statutory rule: exclude capital items](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim42205)
- [BIM42210 — No specific statutory rule: revenue expenditure charged to current assets](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim42210)
- [BIM42215 — Deferred revenue expenditure](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim42215)
