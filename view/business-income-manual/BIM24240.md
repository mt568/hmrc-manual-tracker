title: 'Meaning of trade: mutual trading and members clubs: members'' clubs: different
  forms of club'
---

Members’ clubs can take many forms, unincorporated associations, companies limited by guarantee without share capital and clubs incorporated with a share capital (see [BIM24255](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24255) for specific guidance on members’ clubs having a share capital).

Provided that use of a company structure is simply a means of enabling the members to conduct the social or recreational objectives of a members’ club incorporation does not by itself make any surplus in a members’ club taxable as trade profits.
