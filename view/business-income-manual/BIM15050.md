title: 'Trade profits: statute - main charging provisions'
---

SS5-7 Income (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005 (ITTOIA 2005); S35 Corporation Tax Act 2009 (CTA 2009)

For Income Tax the general charging provisions are in SS5-7 ITTOIA 2005.

S5 ITTOIA 2005 establishes that Income Tax is charged on the profits of a trade, profession or vocation.

S6 ITTOIA 2005 sets out the territorial scope of the charge to tax on trade profits. Details of the section are at [BIM15010](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim15010).

S7(1) ITTOIA 2005 provides that tax is charged ‘on the full amount of the profits for the tax year’. See [BIM15040](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim15040) for further guidance on the meaning of this expression.

Prior to being omitted by FA2022 for the the 2024-25 and subsequent tax years, S7(2) ITTOIA 05 adds that ‘the profits of a tax year are the profits of the basis period for the tax year’. For the rules identifying the basis period for a tax year up to and including the 2022-23 tax year see [BIM81010](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim81010) onwards.

For the 2024-25 tax year onwards basis period rules will not apply, and an individual will instead be taxed on the profits arising in the tax year. There are transitional rules for 2023-24. Please see [BIM81230](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim81230) to [BIM81360](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim81360) for guidance on the transitional year in 2023-24 and [BIM81201](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim81201) for 2024-25 onward.

For Corporation Tax the general charging provisions is S35 CTA 2009 which establishes that Corporation Tax on income applies to the profits of a trade. For the territorial scope of the charge see [BIM15010](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim15010).
