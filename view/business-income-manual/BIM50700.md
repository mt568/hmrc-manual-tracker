title: 'Authors and literary profits: contents'
---

The profits made by individuals resident in the United Kingdom from literary, composing etc activities which they carry on are chargeable to Income Tax either as profits from a profession or as miscellaneous income, if the activities do not amount to the exercise of a profession.
