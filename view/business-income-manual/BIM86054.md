title: "TMIA \u2013 Other Considerations: Capital Gains & Inheritance Tax"
---

The trading allowance does nothing to deny or change the real nature of the activities carried on (trading or receiving miscellaneous income), therefore it will not impact on the Capital Gains Tax and Inheritance Tax provisions which depend on the presence or absence of specific activities at a point in time or over a period.
