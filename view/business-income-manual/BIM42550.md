Where the expenditure is on the erection of permanent hoardings or other apparatus of a durable nature, it is disallowable as capital expenditure.

A payment made to the guarantee fund of an exhibition should be dealt with in accordance with [BIM45305](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim45305).

The deduction of business contributions under collective publicity schemes for a town or area, or contributions to trade publicity funds, is discussed in [BIM47435](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim47435).

For political and propaganda expenses, see [BIM42528](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim42528).
