This chapter deals with the deductions which can be made in calculating the profits of a trade where a premium is paid for a lease of no more than 50 years and the land subject to the lease is used by the tenant in connection with a trade.

For guidance on reverse premiums, see [BIM41050](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim41050) onwards.

The guidance is arranged as follows:
- [BIM46251 — Introduction](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46251)
- [BIM46255 — Calculation of revenue expense](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46255)
- [BIM46260 — Artificial leasing transactions](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46260)
- [BIM46265 — Assignment of lease](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46265)
- [BIM46270 — Tied premises](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46270)
- [BIM46275 — Falkirk & Lennard cases](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46275)
- [BIM46280 — Prevention of double allowance](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46280)
- [BIM46285 — Office procedure](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46285)
