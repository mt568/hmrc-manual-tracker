title: 'Builders, property dealers & developers: valuation: contentious appeal'
---

Before making arrangements for a contentious appeal hearing before the First-tier Tribunal on a trading income valuation point, a report, accompanied by the file, should be made to Business
Profits (Technical).

The report should set out:

* the amount of tax at stake as a result of the disputed valuation, and
* confirm that the principle of using open market value has been accepted by the taxpayer and that it is only the amount of the valuation which is in dispute.
