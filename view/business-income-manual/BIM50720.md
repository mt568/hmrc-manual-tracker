title: 'Authors and literary profits: deductions'
---

In computing the taxable profits of a literary profession or in computing miscellaneous literary income (see [BIM100125](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim100125)), the allowable deductions may include the expenses of producing the literary etc works and the expenses, including agents’ commission, incurred in putting them on the market.
