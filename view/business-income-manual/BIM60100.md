title: 'Measuring the profits (particular trades): land: trading transactions: acquisition
  and disposal of superior interest - approach'
---

The basic trading principles apply in determining whether a tenant’s acquisition and resale of a superior interest is a trading transaction. Common features of such transactions are prearranged or early sales and funding of the purchase to be reimbursed from the proceeds of sale. Such transactions merit close scrutiny to see whether a trading argument is appropriate.
