Part 5 Capital Allowances Act 2001

Mineral Extraction Allowances may be available in respect of capital expenditure in connection with a mineral extraction trade. These are dealt with in detail at [CA50000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/capital-allowances-manual/ca50000) onwards.
