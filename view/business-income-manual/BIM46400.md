title: 'Specific deductions: professional fees: contents'

The chapter that follows discusses the general approach to the deduction of professional fees and covers a number of specific matters on which fees are expended in greater detail. You should bear in mind that professional fees connected with the acquisition, disposal or modification of a capital asset will also be capital expenditure and so disallowable (see [BIM35000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim35000) onwards).
