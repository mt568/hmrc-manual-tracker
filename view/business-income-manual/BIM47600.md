There are a variety of bodies established to assist, support and train businesses. The nature of these bodies and their activities is described below along with the tax treatment of sums that a trader may pay to or receive from such bodies. This chapter covers:
- [BIM47601 — Development boards ](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim47601)
- [BIM47605 — Industrial training boards](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim47605)
- [BIM47610 — Local enterprise organisations and urban regeneration companies](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim47610)
- [BIM47615 — ISO9001 accreditation](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim47615)
