title: 'Measuring the profits (general rules): statutory rules: charging provisions'

The main provisions charging Income Tax and Corporation Tax on trade profits are S5-S7 Income (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005 and S35 Corporation Tax Act 2009 respectively. These provisions are described at [BIM14000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim14000) onwards. See in particular [BIM15040](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim15040) and [BIM15050](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim15050) onwards.
