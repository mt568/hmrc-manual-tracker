title: 'Cash basis: rules not applied in calculating profits'
---

In calculating the profits of a trade using the cash basis,
certain rules do not apply, and some claims and reliefs are not available.

Claims, elections and reliefs not available when using the
cash basis:

* Herd
  basis rules (see [BIM55501](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim55501))
* Averaging
  profits of farmers and creative artists (see [BIM84050](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim84050))
* Compensation
  for compulsory slaughter of animals (see [BIM55180](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim55180))
* Relief
  for unremittable amounts

Special rules not applied in calculating profits on the cash
basis:

* Sound
  recordings (see [BIM56200](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim56200))
* Certain
  telecommunication rights (Chapter 9 or Part 2 ITTOIA 2005)
* Long-funding
  leases (Chapter 10A of Part 2 ITTOIA 2005)
* Changes
  in trading stock (see [BIM33630](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim33630))
* Disposal
  and acquisition of know-how (Chapter 14 of Part 2 ITTOIA 2005)
* Oil
  activities (Chapter 16A of Part 2 ITTOIA 2005)
