title: 'Specific deductions: dilapidations under a lease: contents'
---

Dilapidations are works of repair or re-instatement for which a lessee is liable. Expenditure on dilapidations may be capital or revenue and is allowable or not accordingly.

This chapter contains the following:
- [BIM43251 — Meaning of dilapidations](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim43251)
- [BIM43255 — Capital expenditure](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim43255)
- [BIM43260 — Revenue expenditure](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim43260)
- [BIM43265 — Composition payment to lessor](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim43265)
