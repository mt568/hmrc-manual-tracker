  activities: bulk buying groups - introduction and layout of guidance'

An entity that provides the services of a central buying organisation in order that purchases may be made for members at an advantageous rate may be regarded as trading mutually with those members provided all the other requirements for mutual trading are met - see [BIM24020](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim24020). In such circumstances there is a banding together to buy a product at one price from outsiders and to distribute it at another to members.

The position is different if the body acts as principal rather than merely as agent in relation to such transactions by:

* taking title to the goods, or
* manufacturing the product, or
* growing the product.
