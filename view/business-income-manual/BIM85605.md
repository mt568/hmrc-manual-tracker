title: 'Farming losses: trade loss relief against general income'
---

S66, S67 Income Tax Act 2007

Where a loss is caught by either of the restrictions (1) or (2) at [BIM85601](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim85601):

* trade loss relief against general income is denied in respect of the loss,
* relief against capital gains (see [BIM85025](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim85025)) is similarly denied,
* no restriction is imposed on the right to carry forward unused losses against future profits of the same trade (see [BIM85060](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim85060)).
