This chapter looks at sums received under exclusivity agreements. Under these agreements, traders tie themselves to one supplier of goods or services for a number of years in exchange for a lump sum payment and/or periodic payments.

Information on the tax treatment of the payer can be found at [BIM35550](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim35550) - [BIM35560](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim35560).

This chapter is arranged as follows:
- [BIM40301 — Background](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40301)
- [BIM40305 — Tax and accounting treatment](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim40305)
