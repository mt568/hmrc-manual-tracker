This chapter describes anti-avoidance legislation which applies to certain arrangements for the leasing of plant and machinery and other assets (other than land). It contains the following:
- [BIM61201 — Overview](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61201)
- [BIM61205 — Capital sums](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61205)
- [BIM61210 — Tax-relieved payments](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61210)
- [BIM61215 — Definitions](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61215)
- [BIM61220 — Associated persons](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61220)
- [BIM61225 — Joint interests](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61225)
- [BIM61230 — Additional capital sum: same interests](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61230)
- [BIM61235 — Capital sums examples](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61235)
- [BIM61240 — Leased trading assets](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61240)
- [BIM61245 — Meaning of ‘commercial rent’](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61245)
- [BIM61250 — Calculation of tax deduction](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim61250)
