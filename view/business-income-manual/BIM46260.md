S303 Income Tax (Trading and Other Income) Act 2005, S243 Corporation Tax Act 2009

The aim of these rules is to prevent artificial manipulation of the duration of a lease. The effect is, broadly, to ensure that a lease is treated as ending on the earliest date when it is expected to end to avoid short leases being presented as long leases. See [PIM1206](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/property-income-manual/pim1206) for further details.
