Many forms and help sheets can be downloaded online at Gov.UK. This is the preferred route for customers to take.

For example, [Self-Assessment: forms ordering - GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/find-hmrc-contacts/self-assessment-forms-ordering)

However, order line can be used by customers to order supplementary pages or guidance which has not been enclosed with their paper tax return.

Leaflets or Help Sheets mentioned in the Tax Return Guide can also be ordered from the Order line.

Contacting Orderline:-

* Telephone: 0300 200 3610

* Outside UK: +44 161 930 8331

Opening times: Monday to Friday: 8am to 5pm. Closed [bank holidays](https://www.gov.uk/bank-holidays).
