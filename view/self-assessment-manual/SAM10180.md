title: 'Appeals, postponements and reviews: appeals: appeal acknowledgement form'
---

The following forms are relevant to handling an appeal and should be issued whenever an appeal is received. A copy of the form should be retained with the appeal papers.

| Form | Description |
| --- | --- |
| SA525 | Acknowledgement of an appeal where no application for postponement has been made. / Acknowledgement of an appeal where there is no legal right to postpone, but all or part of the charge is informally stoodover. Note: You should include a reference to interest charged on ‘Outstanding amounts’. |
| SA526 | Acknowledgement of an appeal and a related postponement application which is considered acceptable. |
| SA527(1) | Acknowledgement of an appeal. Alternative proposals made concerning a related postponement application. / Accompanied by form SA527(2) |
| SA527(2) | Acceptance of alternative proposals for a postponement application. To be completed by the appellant. / Issued by HMRC with form SA527(1) |
