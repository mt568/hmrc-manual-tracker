The following work items are dealt with in the Integrated Debt Management System (IDMS)

* ‘Bankruptcy or Voluntary Arrangement’
* ‘Outstanding Debt and / or Return’

The following Debt Management Work Lists are dealt with on SA

* ‘Review Remission for Cancellation’
* ‘Remission and Remission Cancellation Quality Assurance’

Further information about the SA Work Management System is available in section ‘Work Management’ ([SAM71000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/self-assessment-manual/sam71000) onwards). Guidance on clearing the IDMS work items is available in the Debt Management & Banking Manual (DMBM516000).
