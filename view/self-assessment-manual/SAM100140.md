title: 'Records: set up taxpayer record: partnership as partner'
---

In Scotland, a partnership may be a partner in another partnership.

In SA it is not possible for a link to be made between two partnerships records. Therefore the Central Agent Authorisation Team (CAAT), who has responsibility for setting up all partnership records, follow a modified procedure.
