Customers who wish to file their returns online can obtain information from [Gov.uk – file returns online](http://www.gov.uk/log-in-file-self-assessment-tax-return)

Local involvement in dealing with enquiries will generally fall to the office with processing responsibility.

If the enquiry is a request for further information or help you should advise the taxpayer that full details can be found on our Website.

If the taxpayer does not yet have access to the Internet, or they still require more information, they can contact the [Online Services Helpdesk](http://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/hm-revenue-customs/contact/self-assessment-online-services-helpdesk).
