title: 'Records: bankruptcy: notification of bankruptcy'
---

You will receive notification of bankruptcy on form INSOL 200. The form is issued by the Insolvency Claims Handling Unit (ICHU).

**All correspondence in bankruptcy cases should be given urgent attention.**
