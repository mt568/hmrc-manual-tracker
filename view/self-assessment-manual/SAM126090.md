title: 'Returns: filing returns online: structured action requests'

Individuals who have registered, enrolled and activated the SA Online service can contact us securely online and request certain actions in respect of their affairs. They can do this by submitting a Structured Action Request (SAR).

The new or amended information is submitted through a secure online channel and, in addition to the online record being updated, the relevant SA record is also updated and an SA Note added accordingly.

(This content has been withheld because of exemptions in the Freedom of Information Act 2000)

The following actions can be taken from 4 February 2008.

## Individuals

Individuals, and agents authorised to act for them online, can

* Change address (main, capacity, and communication)
* Delete communication details
* Change name (taxpayer or capacity, depending on whether the ACI signal is set)
* Change contact details (day, evening and mobile telephone numbers, fax number and email address)
* Claim for repayment
* Reduce payments on account
* Add, change or delete agents

## Trusts

Trusts, and agents authorised to act for them online, can

* Change address (main, communication, trade and capacity)
* Change main contact details (day, evening and mobile telephone numbers, fax number and email address)
* Claim for repayment
* Reduce payments on account
* Add, change or delete agents

## Partnerships

Partnerships, and agents authorised to act for them online, can

* Change address (main, communication and trade)
* Change contact details (day, evening and mobile telephone numbers, fax number and email address)
* Add, change or delete agents
