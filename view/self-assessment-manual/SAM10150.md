title: 'Appeals, postponements and reviews: appeals: amending appeal details'
---

The only update that can be made to appeal details held on SA is closure using function MAINTAIN APPEAL. You can, however, amend standover details using function MAINTAIN STANDOVERS.

If you find that you have incorrectly entered details of an appeal you must

* Close the appeal

And

* Enter the correct details as a new appeal

When you close an appeal the appropriate entry on the ‘Open Appeals’ Work List will be deleted, providing that any associated formal standover has also been reduced to nil.
