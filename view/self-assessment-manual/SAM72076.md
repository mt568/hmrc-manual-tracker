The Clerical Caseworker user role provides access to all functions required for day to day working in the Recovery Office. The operator with this role is generally responsible for

* Dealing with correspondence
* Maintaining designatory details on the SA taxpayer record
* Recovering SA debts
* Dealing with work items appearing on the following work lists

  + DARM ‘Outstanding Debt and/or Return’
  + ‘Bankruptcy or Voluntary Arrangement Cases’
  + ‘RLS Cases’
  + ‘Potential Transfer Cases’
* Creating and maintaining sundry charges on the SA taxpayer record

The following table shows the functions linked to the role Clerical Caseworker in the Recovery Office.

For details of how to access any of these functions, select ‘Index Of Functions’ on the left of the screen.

On accessing the function, system help is available to you at function, screen, field, and error message levels.
