For appeals against fixed automatic penalties for tax years up to and including 2009-10 from customers dealt with in PT Operations, PAYE, and Self Assessment (PSA), customers are advised to write to

Solicitor’s Office and Legal Services  
HM Revenue and Customs  
BX9 1ZT

Different arrangements apply for Specialist PT and requests for review from customers dealt with by this directorate should not be sent to Howarde House.

For cases within Local Compliance, the reviews will be undertaken by a Local Review Team. Other Business Units will have their own arrangements.

For tax years 2010-11 onwards, for guidance see [SAM61310](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/self-assessment-manual/sam61310).
