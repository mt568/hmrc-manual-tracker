The SA Helpline is a national telephone service providing help, advice and information on a wide range of SA enquiries for taxpayers, agents and employers.

The main help provided includes advice on

* Tax return completion
* Statement and calculation queries
* Interest, penalties and surcharges
* General tax matters

Contact details can be found at [Self Assessment: general enquiries](https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/hm-revenue-customs/contact/self-assessment)
