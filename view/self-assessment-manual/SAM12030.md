title: 'Appeals, postponements and reviews: reviews: review conclusion letters'
---

The review conclusion letters must be sent to the customer when the review is completed and copies sent to the agent, if applicable.

These letters must be addressed to the customer, even if the appeal was made by an agent on behalf of the customer.
