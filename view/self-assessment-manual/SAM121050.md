title: 'Returns - individuals returns: payment received with  individual return'
---

It is important to check whether any payments have been enclosed with the return on the day the return is received.

Payments received should be dealt with on the day of receipt.

Where a payment is received with the return see the Debt Management & Banking Manual(DMBM).
