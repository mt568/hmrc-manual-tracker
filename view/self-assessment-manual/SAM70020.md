The IT Service Centre provides support and problem
resolution for all HMRC IT and telephony services and systems.

To resolve the issue yourself online see  IT,
Phones and Data - index on the intranet where help and
guidance is available on how to solve common problems, plus
answers to frequently asked questions, and much more.

**Alternatively:**

Contact the IT Service Centre by telephone: Search for: IT service centre

Prior to contacting the Service Centre, please have the
following information available.

* Your
  computer name and asset tag – see how to find these

* Any
  error messages on your screen

* Whether
  any colleagues are having the same problem

* Whether
  the problem is intermittent or continuous

Helpdesk advisers can fix many
problems immediately but if they need to investigate your issue
further or refer it to a specialist team, it will take longer.

For an update on a previously reported IT incident, you
should go to My active items in Service Central.

 If you’re unhappy with
how we've handled your issue, you can escalate your Incident or log a complaint
