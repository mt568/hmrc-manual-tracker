Customers
can find information about filing Self Assessment returns online and managing
their tax affairs through their HMRC online account on GOV.UK. If customers are
experiencing technical difficulties, the 'Get help' link on the page
being used will provide further support.

For a quick,
digital way to get help with tax and HMRC services without needing to
contact HMRC directly, customers can also chat with the HMRC digital
assistant [Chat
with HMRC’s digital assistant about tax - GOV.UK](https://www.tax.service.gov.uk/ask-hmrc/chat/online-services-helpdesk)

The digital
assistant is designed to:

* Help customers find information
  about HMRC services.
* Support customers in
  self-serving and completing transactions online.
* Answer common questions using
  automated responses.
* Transfer customers to a webchat
  adviser for more complex queries where that option is available.

 

Further
support and information can also be found on GOV.UK through the following
links:

[Technical
support with HMRC online services - GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/find-hmrc-contacts/technical-support-with-hmrc-online-services)

[HMRC online
services: sign in or set up an account: Sign in or set up an account - GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/log-in-register-hmrc-online-services)
