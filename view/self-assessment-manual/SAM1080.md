  on account and costs'

The taxpayer remains liable for the full costs incurred in distraint or court action where a claim to reduce payments on account is preceded by

* A levy (poinding in Scotland), or
* Judgment (decree in Scotland)

(This content has been withheld because of exemptions in the Freedom of Information Act 2000)

Even where a claim is invalid, see subject ‘Validity of claims to adjust payments on account’ ([SAM1040](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/self-assessment-manual/sam1040)), it may influence the action taken to pursue an unpaid SA liability.
