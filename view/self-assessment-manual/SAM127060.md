title: 'Returns: returns processed at HMRC Wolverhampton: HMRC  Wolverhampton: returns
  which cannot be scanned'
---

Some Short Tax Returns (SA200) will not be suitable for scanning and processing through Automated Data Capture, and they should be captured through Local Data Capture.

Examples include where

* The return is torn or damaged
* There is no barcode, or the barcode has been written on or smudged
* Marks are made on the return

  + Over location markers at the side and top of the form
  + Over the barcode
  + Over page numbers at the base of the form
  + Over the word ‘Your’ on the front page

In these cases, the return should be logged then forwarded to the appropriate office with processing responsibility for manual capture.
