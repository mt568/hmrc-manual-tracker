title: 'Returns: filing paper returns: 31 October deadline: advice for staff on dealing
  with post-dated cheques'
---

The filing date of 31 October for paper returns could bring with it an increase in the number of post dated cheques received with returns for payment on 31 January.

Full guidance on how to handle post dated cheques is in the Debt Management and Banking Manual. See DMBM209130.
