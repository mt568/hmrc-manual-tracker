title: 'Addressing risks and assurance: What are the key risks of the scheme?'
---

The key risk from the scheme is its exploitation for fraud or avoidance. The turnover threshold limits the extent of any unintended leakage, so the key risk might be expressed as use of the scheme by businesses for which it was not intended. [FRS6000](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs6000) holds further details of the anti-avoidance rules in the scheme.
