1. [FRS3300](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs3300) explains whether a business can join the scheme retrospectively.
2. [FRS4100](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs4100) explains whether a business can leave the scheme retrospectively.
3. [FRS6200](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs6200) explains the definition of an associated business.
4. [FRS6500](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs6500) explains what capital expenditure goods are.
5. [FRS6500](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs6500) explains whether the building of an extension qualifies as capital expenditure for the purposes of FRS.
6. [FRS7100](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7100) explains when a business receives a 1% reduction to their flat rate percentage.
