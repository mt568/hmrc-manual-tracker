This A-Z list is sorted by trades and shows the sector to which HMRC considers they belong and the corresponding flat rate percentage. You will find that the sectors are broken down more here, than in the notice.

[FRS7300](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7300) contains an A-Z list of flat rate percentages by sector.

[FRS7700](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7700) contains the flat rates in force between 1 December 2008 and 31 December 2009.

[FRS7800](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7800) contains the flat rates in force between 1 January 2010 and 3 January 2011.
