The VAT Soft Drinks and Industry Levy policy team is responsible for VAT Flat Rate Scheme policy.

If you are seeking policy advice on a particular case that is not covered by this guidance or contained in VAT Notice 733, you should submit either a General Advice Request or a Technical Advice Request. Please see VPOLADV for more information.
