Since the scheme was introduced, some of the flat rate percentages have been amended. The following table contains the flat rates that were in force between 1 January 2010 and 3 January 2011.

[FRS7200](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7200) contains an A-Z list of flat rate percentages by trade.

[FRS7300](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7300) contains an A-Z list of flat rate percentages by sector.

[FRS7700](https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7700) contains the flat rates in force between 1 December 2008 and 31 December 2009.
